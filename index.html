About Our Mandal

Patelwadi Sarvajanik Ganesh Mitramandal has been serving the community for over 25 years with devotion and dedication. Our mission is to celebrate Lord Ganesh's blessings while promoting social harmony, cultural heritage, and community service.

Every year, we bring together hundreds of volunteers and thousands of devotees to celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with grandeur, spirituality, and social responsibility.
