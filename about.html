About Our Mandal

Patelwadi Sarvajanik Ganesh Mitramandal – Serving the community with devotion

Who We Are

Patelwadi Sarvajanik Ganesh Mitramandal has been a cornerstone of our community for over 25 years. Founded with a spirit of devotion and unity, our Mandal organizes one of the most celebrated Ganesh Chaturthi festivals in the area.

Our mission goes beyond celebration — we are committed to social service, cultural preservation, and bringing people together in the spirit of Ganpati Bappa's blessings.

Every year, hundreds of dedicated volunteers come together to make our festival a grand success, ensuring that every devotee leaves with joy and blessings in their heart.
